---

### 4. Post & Engage

**Posting Time**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-9am or 5-6pm NZST

**Engagement Strategy**:
- Respond to first 3-5 comments within 2 hours
- Ask follow-up questions
- Share additional insights
- Tag relevant people when appropriate

**Metrics to Track**:
- Post impressions
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / impressions)
- Profile views
- Link clicks
- New connections/followers

## @ Mentions

Use 2-4 mentions per post. Tag when content directly relates to them.

**Common Mentions**:
- @Medtech Global (PMS/digital health)
- @Health New Zealand - Te Whatu Ora (national health policy)
- @Health Informatics New Zealand (HiNZ) (digital health/informatics)
- @RNZCGP (GP standards/training)
- @General Practice New Zealand (GPNZ) (primary care policy)
- @HealthTech Activator (HTA) (healthtech innovation)
- @New Zealand Doctor / Rata Aotearoa (medical media)

**Full list**: See `linkedin-mentions-hashtags.md` for complete reference.

## # Hashtags

Use 5-8 hashtags per post. Mix evergreen + trending + NZ-specific.

**Recommended Mix** (pick 2-3 from each):

**Evergreen**: #PrimaryCare #GeneralPractice #NZHealth

**Trending**: #AIinHealth #DigitalHealth #HealthTech #HealthInnovation

**NZ-specific**: #AotearoaHealth #GPsNZ #HealthTechNZ #PrimaryCareNZ
