## Usage Guidelines

**Mentions (@)**:
- Use 2-4 mentions per post maximum
- Only tag when content directly relates to them
- Don't over-tag (can look spammy)
- Tag in context (e.g., "As @Medtech Global integrates AI features...")

**Hashtags (#)**:
- Use 5-8 hashtags per post maximum
- Mix evergreen + trending + NZ-specific
- Don't repeat similar hashtags (#HealthTech and #HealthTechNZ are redundant - pick one)
- Place hashtags at the end of the post

**Best Practice**:
- Evergreen hashtags: #PrimaryCare, #GeneralPractice, #NZHealth
- Trending hashtags: #AIinHealth, #DigitalHealth, #HealthTech
- NZ-specific: #AotearoaHealth, #GPsNZ, #HealthTechNZ
- Use 2-3 from each category for balanced reach
